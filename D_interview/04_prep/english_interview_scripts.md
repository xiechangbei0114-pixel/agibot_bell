# 💬 英文面试话术

> 面试价值：⭐⭐⭐（智元有海外业务/英文客户，面试可能冒英文）
> 准备：仅需3分钟量，不是重点

---

## 一、30秒英文自我介绍（Mini Intro）

> *"My name is [Name], a solution engineer with 3 years of hands-on experience in 3C manufacturing at Goertek. I built AI vision inspection systems deployed across 20+ production lines, developed an AGV warehouse inventory solution, and led a cross-border delivery project in Vietnam. I hold a PMP certification and a master's degree in communications from NUAA. I'm now looking to bring my industrial know-how to embodied AI — specifically helping customers see clear ROI from humanoid robot deployments."*

---

## 二、方案讲解（3分钟 — 龙旗案例）

> *"Let me walk you through our flagship case — Longcheer Technology, a 3C ODM manufacturer in Nanchang.*
>
> *The challenge: their tablet testing station required 12 operators per shift for pick-and-place work. High turnover, inconsistent quality.*
>
> *Our solution: deployed 4 G2 humanoid robots, each with a 26-DOF semi-humanoid design, IP42 rated, 100% automotive-grade. The architecture is a 4-tier fallback system: VLA end-to-end → vision retry → force control → manual alarm.*
>
> *Key metrics: peak UPH 376, average 296, first-pass yield 99.91%. The robots run 24/7 with MTBF improved from hours to hundreds of hours. Total investment ¥2.28M, annual net cash flow ¥1.888M, payback period only 1.21 years.*
>
> *What made this work was Genie Sim — 90% of R&D done in simulation — plus a structured cold-start process: simulation → sub-line → main line stress test → full production. New skills reach 99%+ success in 1 hour.*
>
> *This model is replicable. We're now discussing similar deployments with 3 other 3C manufacturers."*

## 四、面试开场白

**面试官问："Can you walk me through your experience?"**

> *"Sure. My background is a blend of industrial manufacturing and AI — which is exactly what a solution role in embodied AI needs.*
>
> *For the past 3 years at Goertek, I've been responsible for deploying AI vision solutions on 3C production lines: cameras, YOLO models, MES integration, and dealing with real-world problems like bad lighting, reflective surfaces, and production line vibration. I also built an AGV warehouse inventory system from scratch — AGV navigation + multi-camera coordination + automated reporting.*
>
> *Later I led a product development for an AI inspection agent, where I learned an important lesson: in industrial settings, models alone aren't enough. You need workflow engines to constrain LLM output, rule-based fallbacks for edge cases, and environmental controls to reduce model complexity. This 'engineering resilience' thinking is directly transferable to VLA system design.*
>
> *Now I want to apply this experience to embodied AI — helping industrial customers understand where humanoid robots deliver the highest ROI, and designing solutions that actually work on the factory floor."*
